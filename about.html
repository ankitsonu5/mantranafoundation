About the Foundation

A step towards entrepreneurship and self-reliance

Entrepreneurship & Vision of Progress

MANTRANA FOUNDATION was established in 2025 with the objective of promoting entrepreneurship and self-reliance in society. As a non-governmental organization (NGO), we believe in connecting those people and groups to the mainstream who are left behind due to lack of resources or information.

The main goal of the foundation is to spread awareness about various government schemes such as Startup India, Digital MSME, and other Self-Help Group (SHG) programs. We ensure that an effective marketing system is developed for the products of small entrepreneurs and groups.

We are continuously working to provide a strong identity to MSMEs and self-help groups at national and global levels through both online and offline platforms. This includes product promotion, branding, exhibitions, modern digital marketing, and developing a complete network to connect directly with markets.

MANTRANA FOUNDATION is not just an organization, but a commitment to public awareness and progress for society and enterprises.
